VEXANA
Vexana, the Shimmer of Hope, is a hero in Mobile Legends:
Bang Bang, and is accompanied by Undead Knight.
Vexana was the majestic ruler of Necrokeep,
the gentle mother of the undead,but also the only glimmer of light
that penetrated the Mist, bringing hope to this cursed
land shunned by both life and death.
